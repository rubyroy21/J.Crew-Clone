Friends don't let friends wear boring tops and blouses. And right now, we're all about awesome women's tops and shirts—from of-the-moment off-the-shoulder tops (that go great with your most dramatic dangly earrings) to casual popover shirts. Fact: A fun new top is the fastest way to make your favorite wear-them-every-single-day pair of jeans feel exciting again. Just add a shirt with a pretty flourish, like ruffles, peplums and bell-shaped short sleeves or long sleeves. Looking for something a little more polished that you can pair with black pants and pencil skirts for work? We've got lots and lots of office-friendly dress shirts, button-up women's blouses in classic gingham, colorful plaids and a few unexpected prints (berries, anyone?). There are so many tops for women to choose from that you might even say we went a little over the top...cymbal crash!
